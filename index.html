Meet Aadil

Aadil Yousuf is a proud graduate of FLHS, Class of 2023. He captained the varsity basketball team for two years and worked with the Mayor to organize community events in Fair Lawn. He’s now at Rutgers Business School with a full scholarship and leadership roles in student government, consulting, and finance organizations.

With experience at Johnson & Johnson and the National Basketball Association, Aadil brings a fresh, yet experienced perspective to educational leadership.

Why I’m Running

I’ve seen what students need—and what’s missing. Fair Lawn needs leadership that evolves with the times. I’m running to bring clarity, transparency, and care to every school decision. I’m close enough to understand what students face, and old enough to make change happen.

My Vision for Fair Lawn Students

Support Beyond Graduation

Appoint a Career Chair to support students after graduation—through jobs, mentorship, and life planning.

Make the Budget Clear

Provide a transparent, visual public budget so every taxpayer knows exactly where their money goes.

Invest in Student Technology

Upgrade learning environments with modern tools and tech that meet today's digital learning standards.
